About Kenneth

Tanzania Is Not
Just a Destination —
It's a Feeling

Born and raised in Dar es Salaam, I grew up where the Indian Ocean meets the African heartland. For over a decade, I've been crafting immersive journeys that go beyond the ordinary tourist trail — deep into the Serengeti at golden hour, across the spice-scented streets of Zanzibar's Stone Town, and out onto the water as dhows glide through violet dusk.

My expertise is in reading the land and connecting people to its rhythms. Every tour is tailored, every moment is intentional. I hold certifications from the Tanzania Tourist Board and speak English, Swahili, and basic German to serve my international guests best.

Where We Go

Signature Destinations

Northern Tanzania

The Serengeti

Over 30,000 sq km of unbroken savannah. Home to two million wildebeest, the wildebeest migration, and predators who rule the land. I'll position you at the crossings, at the kills, at the golden-hour panoramas that last forever in memory.

Northern Circuit

Ngorongoro Crater

The world's largest intact volcanic caldera — a self-contained Eden with elephants, rhinos, flamingos, and every shade of green. The fog rolls off the rim at dawn in a way that feels primordial and sacred.

Southern Tanzania

Nyerere National Park

Africa's largest game reserve, mostly untouched and truly wild. Boat safaris on the Rufiji River bring you face-to-face with hippos and crocs. Walking safaris here are not for the faint-hearted — they're for the genuinely curious.

Dar es Salaam

The Harbour City

Kariakoo Market, Msasani Peninsula, the National Museum, incredible Swahili coastal cuisine, and sunset dhow rides. The city that never stops surprising — and the perfect start or finish to any Tanzanian adventure.

Island Chapter

Zanzibar —
Where Time Drifts
Like Spice on the Wind

The "Spice Island" is a world apart — Arab doors carved in centuries-old wood, fishermen mending nets at sunrise, turquoise water over coral reefs, and the scent of cloves drifting over everything.
